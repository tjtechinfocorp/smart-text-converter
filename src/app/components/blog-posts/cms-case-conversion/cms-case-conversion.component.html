Post Title Formatting Strategies

WordPress Title Case Best Practices:

Blog Post Titles: "How to Optimize Your WordPress Site for Better Performance"
Page Titles: "About Our Company: Leading Digital Solutions Provider"
Product Titles: "Premium WordPress Theme: Modern Business Design"
Category Titles: "Web Development Tutorials and Best Practices"
  • Use title case for all post and page titles to maintain professionalism
  • Capitalize major words: nouns, verbs, adjectives, and adverbs
  • Lowercase articles (a, an, the), conjunctions (and, but, or), and prepositions
  • Always capitalize the first and last words regardless of type
