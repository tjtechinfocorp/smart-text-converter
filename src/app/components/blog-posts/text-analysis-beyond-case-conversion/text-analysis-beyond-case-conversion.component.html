Sentiment Analysis and Emotional Intelligence

Understanding Text Emotion and Tone

Sentiment analysis goes beyond word counting to understand the emotional context and tone of your content. Research shows that emotionally engaging content receives 3x more shares and 2x more engagement than neutral content, making sentiment analysis crucial for content optimization.

Sentiment Scoring Methods

Advanced Sentiment Analysis Techniques:

Polarity Analysis:
• Positive: +0.8 to +1.0
• Neutral: -0.2 to +0.2
• Negative: -1.0 to -0.8
Emotion Detection:
• Joy, Sadness, Anger
• Fear, Surprise, Disgust
• Trust, Anticipation

Advanced Readability Formulas

Comprehensive Readability Assessment:
