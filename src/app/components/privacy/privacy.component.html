Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Smart Text Converter.

Last updated: September 22, 2025

1. Introduction

Smart Text Converter ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices when you visit our website smarttextconverter.com and use our text conversion services. Important: All text processing happens locally in your browser - we do not collect, store, or have access to your text content.

By using our service, you agree to our data practices as described in this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the site.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Text Content

Local Processing Only: All text conversion happens entirely in your browser. We do not store, transmit, or have access to the text you input into our tools. Your text never leaves your device.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide and maintain our text conversion services

  • User Experience: To enhance user interface and functionality
  • Security: To protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Communication: To respond to user inquiries and provide support
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • No Text Storage: Your text content is never stored on our servers
  • Secure Hosting: Our website is hosted on secure, reliable infrastructure
  • Regular Updates: We keep our systems updated with the latest security patches
  • Access Controls: Limited access to any collected data on a need-to-know basis
